Laser Hair Removal: Everything You Should Know

Laser hair removal is one of the most effective and long-term solutions for unwanted hair. At California Aesthetic, treatments are performed using advanced technology for safe and visible results.

What Is Laser Hair Removal?

Laser hair removal uses concentrated light energy to target hair follicles. The pigment in the hair absorbs the light, which damages the follicle and slows future hair growth.

Benefits of Laser Hair Removal

  • Long-lasting hair reduction
  • Smoother skin texture
  • Reduced ingrown hairs
  • Suitable for multiple body areas

Is It Safe?

When performed by trained professionals, laser hair removal is safe and effective. Skin type, hair type, and medical history are always assessed before treatment.

How Many Sessions Are Needed?

Most clients require multiple sessions spaced a few weeks apart to achieve optimal results, as hair grows in cycles.

If you’re considering laser hair removal, consult with our experts at California Aesthetic to determine the best plan for your skin.
